About

The Artist

Elena Vasquez is a Spanish-born painter currently based in Barcelona, whose work explores the intersection of light, memory, and landscape. Drawing on the luminous tradition of Sorolla while pushing toward contemporary abstraction, her paintings capture fleeting moments of natural beauty rendered in oil and watercolor.

After completing her MFA at the Royal College of Art, London, Elena returned to the Mediterranean coast where the quality of light first shaped her artistic vision. Her paintings have been exhibited across Europe and the Americas, and are held in private and institutional collections worldwide.
